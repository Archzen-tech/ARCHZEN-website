Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 7 July 2026

At ArchZen Pty Ltd ("ArchZen", "we", "our", or "us"), protecting your privacy and safeguarding your information is fundamental to the way we do business.

As a provider of Managed IT Services, Cybersecurity, Cloud Solutions, AI Services, Business Process Automation, and Professional IT Consulting, we understand the importance of maintaining the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information entrusted to us.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, store, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, engage our services, or otherwise interact with us.

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

ArchZen is committed to protecting your privacy and handling personal information responsibly.

Where applicable, we aim to manage personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

Privacy and security form an integral part of our service delivery and business operations.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on your interaction with us, we may collect the following information:

Personal Information

  • Full name
  • Company name
  • Job title
  • Business email address
  • Phone number
  • Business address
  • Billing information
  • Support contact information

Technical Information

When you visit our website or use our services, we may collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Operating system
  • Website usage statistics
  • Cookies and similar technologies
  • Pages visited
  • Referral source
  • Session information

Service Information

Where applicable, we may collect information required to deliver our services, including:

  • Microsoft 365 tenant information
  • Google Workspace information
  • Cloud infrastructure details
  • Network configuration
  • Device inventory
  • Security logs
  • Support requests
  • Project documentation

We only collect information necessary to provide our services effectively.

3. How We Collect Information

We collect information in several ways, including:

  • When you submit a contact form
  • When requesting a quotation
  • During meetings and consultations
  • Through support requests
  • During onboarding
  • Through service agreements
  • When subscribing to newsletters
  • Through cookies and website analytics
  • During delivery of managed IT services

Some information is collected automatically when you use our website.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to enquiries
  • Provide Managed IT Services
  • Deliver Cybersecurity services
  • Implement AI and automation solutions
  • Provide technical support
  • Prepare quotations and proposals
  • Deliver projects
  • Manage customer accounts
  • Process invoices and payments
  • Improve our services
  • Improve website performance
  • Send important service notifications
  • Send educational content and cybersecurity updates (where permitted)
  • Comply with legal obligations

We only use information for legitimate business purposes.

5. AI Services

ArchZen provides Artificial Intelligence and Business Automation services. Where AI technologies are used in delivering services:

  • AI is used to assist our consultants and improve efficiency.
  • Human oversight is maintained for business-critical work.
  • Client confidentiality remains our priority.
  • We do not intentionally use confidential client information to train public AI models.
  • AI-generated outputs should be reviewed before being relied upon for important business decisions.

Additional information is available in our AI Usage Policy.

6. Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Improve website functionality
  • Remember user preferences
  • Analyse website traffic
  • Measure website performance
  • Improve user experience

Most web browsers allow you to manage or disable cookies through browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain website functionality.

7. Marketing Communications

From time to time, we may send:

  • Business updates
  • Cybersecurity alerts
  • Educational articles
  • Technology insights
  • Service announcements
  • Event invitations

You may unsubscribe at any time using the unsubscribe link included in our communications or by contacting us directly. We promptly honour all unsubscribe requests.

8. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent personal information.

We may disclose information where necessary to trusted service providers who assist in delivering our services. Examples may include:

  • Microsoft
  • Google
  • Cloudflare
  • HubSpot
  • Datto
  • Payment providers
  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Communication platforms

These providers are expected to maintain appropriate security and privacy standards. We may also disclose information where required by law.

9. International Data Transfers

Some of our technology providers operate globally. As a result, your information may be processed or stored in countries outside Australia.

Where this occurs, we take reasonable steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information.

10. Data Security

Protecting information is central to our business. We implement appropriate administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to reduce the risk of unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Security measures may include:

  • Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
  • Encryption where appropriate
  • Secure cloud infrastructure
  • Role-based access controls
  • Secure backup solutions
  • Security monitoring
  • Endpoint protection
  • Patch management
  • Secure remote access
  • Staff security awareness

Although we take reasonable measures to protect information, no method of transmission or storage can guarantee absolute security.

11. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary to deliver our services, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, maintain business records, and support ongoing client relationships.

When information is no longer required, it is securely deleted or destroyed where reasonably practicable.

12. Access and Correction

You may request access to personal information we hold about you. If you believe any information is inaccurate, incomplete, or outdated, you may request that it be corrected.

Reasonable verification may be required before processing requests.

13. Third-Party Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. These websites operate independently of ArchZen. We are not responsible for their privacy practices or content. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of any external websites they visit.

14. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for businesses and individuals aged 18 years or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

15. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in legislation, industry standards, technology, business operations, and service offerings.

The latest version will always be published on our website.

16. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, wish to access your personal information, or would like to make a privacy-related enquiry, please contact us through our Contact page.
